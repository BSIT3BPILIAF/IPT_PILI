My Services

Web Design

Web design involves creating the layout, visual appearance, and usability of websites. It focuses on the aesthetics (colors, typography, images) and user experience (navigation, interaction). Web designers use tools like Adobe XD, Figma, and Sketch to design responsive websites that work across different devices (desktop, mobile, tablet).

Web Development

Web development refers to the creating, building, and maintaining of websites. It includes aspects such as web design, web publishing, web programming, and database management. It is the creation of an application that works over the internet i.e. websites.

SEO

SEO is the practice of optimizing websites to rank higher in search engine results, increasing organic (non-paid) traffic. It involves on-page strategies (like keyword optimization, meta tags, and content quality) and off-page s trategies (like backlinks and social signals). SEO also includes technical SEO, ensuring the site is crawlable, fast, and mobile-friendly.

Content Writting

Content writing involves creating written material for websites, blogs, social media, and other digital platforms. It’s focused on delivering valuable, engaging, and informative content that appeals to the target audience. Good content writing also includes SEO practices to help the content rank well in search engines.

Wordpress Dev

WordPress development refers to building and customizing websites using the WordPress content management system (CMS). It includes creating themes, plugins, and website functionalities, making WordPress flexible for users with little technical expertise. Developers can use PHP, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create custom solutions

Video Editing

Video editing involves manipulating and arranging video footage to create a polished final product. This can include cutting, adding effects, transitions, music, and sound effects. Video editors use software like Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or DaVinci Resolve to create videos for platforms such as YouTube, social media, or advertisements.
